Privacy Policy – Blockit

Last updated: [DATE]

This Privacy Policy explains how Blockit (the “App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App.

Blockit is operated by an independent developer (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Contact: contact@blockit.app

Quick Summary

  • Exercise detection is processed locally on your device.
  • We do not record, store, or upload camera images or videos.
  • App blocking and restrictions are enforced locally on your device.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Information We Collect

On-device processing: Blockit uses your device camera to analyze movements for exercise repetition counting (such as push-ups). This processing is performed locally on your device. Raw camera frames or videos are not stored or transmitted by us.

Usage and diagnostics: We may collect limited, non-identifying usage and diagnostic information (such as app version, device type, or crash logs) to improve performance and stability.

Subscription information: Purchase status and subscription information are provided by Apple in order to verify access to premium features. We do not have access to your payment details.

Support communications: If you contact us, we may collect the information you choose to provide.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Enable exercise detection and app restriction features
  • Verify subscriptions and premium access
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only with Apple as necessary for subscription management, or when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to operate the App. Exercise motion data used for repetition counting is not stored as raw camera or video content.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Blockit is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
